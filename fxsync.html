Configuring Firefox Sync


Configure Firefox Desktop:

1. Log out of the official Firefox Sync service.
2. Go to about:config
3. Search for identity.sync.tokenserver.uri
4. Change the value to https://fxsync.rootiest.dev/token/1.0/sync/1.5
5. Restart Firefox and log in to your Firefox Sync as normal.
6. Login is provided by Mozilla but sync services will be carried out by Rootiest Solutions.
7. Enjoy!

Configure Fennec F-Droid:

1. Log out of the official Firefox Sync service.
2. Open the Fennec F-Droid settings.
3. Scroll to the bottom and select About Fennec
4. Tap the Fennec logo 5-10 times to enable debug settings.
5. Go back to the main settings page at the top and select Sync Debug
6. Tap Custom Sync Server and enter https://fxsync.rootiest.dev/token/1.0/sync/1.5
7. Restart Fennec and log in to your Firefox Sync as normal.
8. Login is provided by Mozilla but sync services will be carried out by Rootiest Solutions.
9. Enjoy!

Configuring Firefox Sync


Firefox:

Log out of the official Firefox Sync service. Go to about:config Search for identity.sync.tokenserver.uri Change the value to https://fxsync.rootiest.dev/token/1.0/sync/1.5 Restart Firefox and log in to your Firefox Sync as normal. Login is provided by Mozilla but sync services will be carried out by Rootiest Solutions. Enjoy!
